Generative Wordmark
2.0s
A — Generative Drift

Each letter independently drifts to a new random weight + width on its own staggered timer. The wordmark is never the same twice — always shifting, always unique. This is the core concept.

B — Layered Echo

Three stacked instances of JUANEMO, each with different animation speeds. The top layer leads, the others follow at delay — creating a typographic echo. The ghost layers use faded colors.

C — Proximity + Drift

Generative drift runs as ambient behavior, but letters near your cursor get pulled toward bold + extended. Move away and they relax back to their random target. Two systems layered: generative + interactive.

D — Freeze Frame

Click any letter to freeze it in its current state (turns Bittersweet). Click again to release. You're composing your own version of the wordmark by choosing which moments to keep. A collaborative generative system.
