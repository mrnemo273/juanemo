About This Section
Each letter of JUANEMO independently randomizes its weight, width, and optical size on a timed hold → shift cycle. The result is a wordmark that never looks the same twice — a living identity that drifts through the variable font's 13-axis design space.
Watch — the letters shift automatically on a timed cycle. Each character moves independently.
Shuffle — tap the button below to instantly re-randomize all letters at once.
Speed — change how long letters hold before shifting. Slower speeds feel meditative; faster feels energetic.
