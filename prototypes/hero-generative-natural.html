Generative Wordmark
2.0s
A — Generative Drift

Each letter independently drifts to a new random weight + width on its own staggered timer. The wordmark is never the same twice — always shifting, always unique.

B — Layered Echo

Three stacked instances. The top leads, the others follow at delay — typographic afterimage. Ghost layers use fading colors.

C — Proximity + Drift

Ambient generative drift, but letters near your cursor get pulled toward bold + extended. Move away and they relax back. Two systems layered.

D — Freeze Frame

Click any letter to freeze it (turns Bittersweet). Click again to release. Compose your own version of the wordmark.
